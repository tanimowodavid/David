RetireSmart

Retirement Awareness Tool


This app is a personal finance companion designed to help people take control of their financial future. With just a few details — your current age, income, spending, savings, retirement age, and an estimate of inflation — the app calculates how much you’ll need to retire comfortably and how much you should be saving today to get there.

But it doesn’t stop at the numbers. To make the experience more engaging and insightful, the app uses AI to generate personalized financial advice, tailored to your lifestyle and goals. It even recommends a relevant book to help you deepen your understanding of money and retirement planning.

The app visualizes your journey with a projection chart that shows how inflation will affect your spending over time, making it clear why consistent saving and planning matter. With progress bars, clean results cards, and structured advice formatted for readability, the app transforms what could be a stressful calculation into an interactive, motivating experience.

Built with Flask and deployed on Render, this project showcases my ability to create user-centric applications that combine practical functionality with modern design and AI integration. It’s a tool that not only informs but also inspires users to take proactive steps towards a secure financial future.
